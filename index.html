Welcome to SongPushify.

The fastest and easiest way of discovering new music with your friends on a social network like no other. Unlike any other music app, SongPushify provides a specialized social song network where people can find out about great music quickly and easily. Oh, and did we mention it comes with music recognition also?

Discover New Music Even Faster than Before

With SongPushify, you'll be able to discover new music on the public music cloud social network. By following different song clouds, you will receive tailored notifications that will help you discover your new favorite songs quicker and easier.

Music Recognition Technology

SongPushify includes music recognition technology to help discover new music on your own. In addition, you'll also be able to post and share these newly recognized songs to the public song clouds or even save it to your own private playlist.
